## TRANSLATION GUIDELINES

### Tone & Voice Requirements
- **Original Tone:** Informal, playful, academic-casual mix, uses humor
- **Target Tone:** Maintain playful academic tone, keep humor, preserve cultural nuance
- **Formality:** Casual for UI, formal for legal/academic sections

### Special Terminology
- **גיחוך (Gichuch):** Portmanteau of גיחוך (laugh) + אדריכלות (architecture)
  - Context-dependent translation needed
  - Suggestions: "Meme", "Gigglechitecture", "ArchMeme", "Architectural Critique"

- **מכונת הגיחוך וההגחה:** "The Gigglechitecture Machine" or similar playful brand name

### Critical Preservation Rules
1. **Variables:** Keep all `{variable}` and `${variable}` placeholders EXACTLY as shown
2. **Line Breaks:** Preserve `\n` where indicated
3. **Emoji:** Keep all emoji characters (🎉, 📧, etc.)
4. **Icons:** Icon names (Home, Mail, etc.) are for reference only - don't translate
5. **Asterisks:** Preserve `*` for required field indicators
