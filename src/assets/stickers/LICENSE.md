Twitter, Inc. and other contributors, licensed under
[CC-BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).
Source: https://github.com/jdecked/twemoji (the maintained fork).

Filenames are the emoji's Unicode codepoints, matching the upstream asset
names, so individual files can be traced back to their source.
